## Usage Guide

### Light Mode (Beige)
- **Use for**: Daytime, well-lit rooms
- **Feeling**: Warm, professional, calm
- **Best with**: Natural light
- **Eye strain**: Low

### Dark Mode (Grey)
- **Use for**: Night, dark rooms
- **Feeling**: Modern, sleek, focused
- **Best with**: Low light
- **Eye strain**: Very low
